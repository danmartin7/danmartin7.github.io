ENG 101 Outcomes

ENG 101 is built on five domains of knowledge: Rhetorical, writing process, genre, discourse community,and content knowledge.

The Stretch Program


Study and practice in critical reading, critical writing, and information literacy with an emphasis on learning how writing is a recursive process that is rhetorically situated.
